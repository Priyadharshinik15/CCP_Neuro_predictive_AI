Creutzfeldt-Jakob Disease (CJD)

Overview: A rare, fatal brain disorder caused by prions.

Symptoms: Rapid dementia, vision loss, jerks, stiffness.

Diagnosis: EEG, MRI, CSF test.

Treatment: No cure, supportive care only.
